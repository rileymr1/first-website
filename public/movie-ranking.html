The Best Movies According to Michael

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Lego Batman Movie

This movie is funny, there's lots of inside jokes about batman lore, and it's about a loner learning to love a family again.

The Dark Knight

Again, incredibly well-written and paced film with excellent performances and themes.

The Incredibles

A perfect film. Story, writing, everything. And perfect timing with my own childhood.
